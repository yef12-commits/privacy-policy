Privacy Policy for The Global Counter

Last updated: March 17, 2026

The Global Counter ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect only the username you choose to enter in the app. This username is stored in our database to display on the leaderboard.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

Firebase

We use Google Firebase to store usernames, tap counts, and leaderboard data. Firebase may collect anonymous usage analytics.

Data Retention

Your username and tap count are stored until you delete the app or request deletion by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
